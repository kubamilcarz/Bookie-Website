<Callout type="info" title="Quick overview">
  Bookie ships on the **Apple App Store** for iPhone and iPad. The flow below mirrors what you
  see on device, so you can follow along step by step.
</Callout>

<TwoColumn>
  <div>
    <h2>Before you start</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Apple ID signed in on the device</li>
      <li>Stable Wi-Fi or 5G connection</li>
      <li>~75 MB of free storage</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div>
    <Callout type="tip" title="System requirements">
      Bookie supports iOS/iPadOS 16 and above. On older devices you can still export data from the
      Settings page on another device and import later.
    </Callout>
  </div>
</TwoColumn>

## 1. Grab Bookie from the App Store

<StepList>
  <li>Open the <strong>App Store</strong> on your iPhone or iPad.</li>
  <li>Search for <strong>"Bookie Reading Tracker"</strong>.</li>
  <li>Tap <strong>Get</strong> (or the download cloud if you've installed before).</li>
  <li>Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.</li>
</StepList>

## 2. Launch and sign in

You'll land on a short onboarding flow that highlights reading goals, quote capture, and reading stats.

<QuickTip>
  Already on Bookie for iPad? Tap <strong>Sign in with Apple</strong> to keep your reading progress fully in sync.
</QuickTip>

<StepList>
  <li>Choose <strong>Continue with Apple</strong> or create a Bookie account with email.</li>
  <li>Grant notification access (optional but recommended for gentle nudges).</li>
  <li>Enable <strong>iCloud Sync</strong> so reading data follows you across devices.</li>
</StepList>

## 3. Add your first book

Tap <strong>Add book</strong> from the bottom nav to scan an ISBN, import from Apple Books, or type manually.
